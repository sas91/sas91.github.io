Aswin Shanmugam Subramanian

PhD Student

Johns Hopkins University

I am a graduate student at Johns Hopkins University, working in the Center for Language and Speech Processing (CLSP), advised by Prof. Shinji Watanabe. My research interests are in speech denoising, speech dereverberation, source separation, multi-source localization, robust speech recognition and end-to-end speech recognition.
